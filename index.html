Welcome to epistemo leadership school

Vision & mission

Epistemo has derived its name from a Greek word, which means knowledge, and intends to pave a new path in the field of education to explore newer dimensions of effective learning. The endeavour is to kindle the spark of inquisitiveness in a child and shine the light of knowledge bright.

Overview

Epistemo Vikas Leadership School is a part of Samaikya Educations, established with the main objective of nurturing young minds by imparting training, dissemination of knowledge, know-how with the most innovative and proven methodologies of teaching.
